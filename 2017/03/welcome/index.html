Welcome

March 2017 · 1 minute read

A post shared by Prasanna Srivatsav (@vogxn) on Jan 24, 2017 at 6:26pm PST

Greetings - I’m a software developer based in Bangalore, India. Many years ago I wrote blogs while going through college at the IIIT-H. As life took over and the dayjob paid the bills, I lost touch with blogging. Back then blogging was a big deal and I used to read the ilks of Meghalomania and Krish Ashok. Facebook and Twitter soon killed away the gusto of bloggers but given the vitriolic atmosphere of these platforms long-form makes a lot more sense now. It’s the return of the bloggers!

This blog is meant to express my thoughts about software, politics, india, photography, music, cooking, gardening, people-being-wrong-on-the-internet and so on.
